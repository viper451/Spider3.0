About Us

When people think of ice cream they think of happiness,joy comfort and love. We being inspired by these emotions set off on a fun filled journey of creating magic in the form of ice-cream We aim at providing the best quality if ice cream in the most hygiene way and offered at an attractive price point.

Our ice cream is made using a 15% butterfat mix,natural product,organic, which makes it a much creamier, richer, and more delicious product. This greatly exceeds the minimum requirement of 10% in order to legally be called ice cream, and you will be able to tell by the taste and texture that it is far superior to anything labeled “frozen dairy dessert”.

Our ice cream production facility is located 23 kilometer from the DharuKhana family farm(Mumbai). We are a team which maufactures and produce ice cream 4 times a week making us one of the largest producer of ice cream in the firm We make sure that our employee our located closely,if not we provide them PG to stay.We currently have 2 branch one in Lower Parel and other one in Mira Road.
